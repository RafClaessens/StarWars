The shinobi beginning (Naruto)

Naruto is a story about a boy, a resident of the Hidden Leaf Village, who witnessed pain since he was born and strives hard to achieve recognition in his own village by vowing to himself to become the village leader. He shares a friendly relation with another kid Sasuke who is a genius unlike him who wishes to avenge the death of his clan by killing his elder brother. The first arc get over when Sasuke abandoned the Hidden Leaf village in order to grow stronger and Naruto vows to bring him back. At this time he finds out that an evil group Akatsuki is after him due to some evil spirit sealed inside him. This is where 'Naruto' ends as he leaves to train in order to become strong.
